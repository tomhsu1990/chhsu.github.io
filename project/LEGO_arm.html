LEGO Robot Arm System

    Course: Introduction to Robotics, Department of Mechanics, National Taiwan Univeristy
    Date: Oct. 2012 - Jan. 2013

Our self-built LEGO robot arm has 3 joints. Its application is for writing. I worked with two students majored in Mechanical Engineering to build it from scratch. I was in charge of formulating the path equation for doing Inverse Kinematics and programming in NXT language. One of difficult problems we faced is that after disassembling and assembling the arm, the operation of the gears is not consistent. In other words, it is not so accurate. To cope with this situation, we experiment and add more control points to solve this problem.
