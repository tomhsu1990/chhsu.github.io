3D Pop-up Book

    Advised by: Dr. Ruen-Rone Lee
    Date: Sep. 2010 - Aug. 2011

3D Pop-up Book is a book rendering engine on portable devices. Through our engine's help, authors can edit and design various vivid 3D book contents for readers. The commercial idea is to have readers not only download author's design, but also download our platform. With our reading engine's friendly interaction interface and smooth folding effect, readers can enjoy author's contents in a different way.

The most challenging part is that it is an android project during 2010-2011. At that time, most smart phones has around 1 GHz CPU performance 1, not to mention some smart phones only run with 500 MHz CPU. The performance is a big issue! We kept optimizing the algorithm by not only using swap buffer but also pre-load and pre-process pages of the book and simplifying our design.
